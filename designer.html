My Design Journey

I have a degree in Communication Arts from NYIT, and I was introduced to design software there - photoshop and after effects primarily.

It wasn't until the Covid-19 pandemic however, when I began to study design in earnest. I learned about typography, color theory, layouts, and more. I worked for a few local businesses, and I lended my skills to a non-profit.

The aspect of design that I really love is photo manipulation. I loved making collages as a kid, and photo manipulation is just an extreme version of that.
